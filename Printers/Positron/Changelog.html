Positron V3.2

This is the latest revision of The Positron Printer, designed by Positron 3D, Positron LT, and LDO in collaboration.

Significant Changes from the V3;

  1. Guided extrusion cut for folding hinge, ensuring reliable folding/unfolding.
  2. 3mm belts replaced Synchromesh for easier maintenance and sourcing
  3. Bowden Extruder upgraded to an LDO Orbiter style extruder
  4. Custom heatblock provided by LDO, V6 compatible.
  5. Custom PCBs for MCU and PTH designed and provided by LDO
  6. Completely Clear ITO Coated Heated Glass Bed
  7. Metal Idlers instead of Synchromesh Specced Plastic Idlers.
  8. External Display Connected to MCU by USB C, containing the RPi CM4 externally.
  9. Added cutout for a internal power supply (future upgrade option)
  10. Added new supporting extrusion to prevent filament scraps from getting inside the printer.
  11. Added sentient catgirl A.I.
