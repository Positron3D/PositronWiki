Positron v3.2

The current version of the Positron printer. There have been rolling updates since the release of v3.2, but with each batch of kits released there have been milestone updates. The dates correspond with the approximate date that kits were received.

Positron v3.2.1 (Batch 2)

Released: September 2024

We are currently experimenting with a minor revision. CAD will be published after further experimentation.

Significant changes from v3.2.0:

  • Z-Endstop on Extruder Assembly
  • New Custom designed fan duct from Olli3D
  • Added sentient catboi A.I.

Positron v3.2.0 (Batch 1)

Released: June 2024

This is the first release of The Positron printer kit, designed by Positron 3D, Positron LT, and LDO in collaboration.

Significant Changes from the V3:

  • Guided extrusion cut for folding hinge, ensuring reliable folding/unfolding.
  • 3mm belts replaced Synchromesh for easier maintenance and sourcing
  • Bowden Extruder upgraded to an LDO Orbiter style extruder
  • Custom heatblock provided by LDO, V6 compatible.
  • Custom PCBs for MCU and PTH designed and provided by LDO
  • Completely Clear ITO Coated Heated Glass Bed
  • Metal Idlers instead of Synchromesh Specced Plastic Idlers.
  • External Display Connected to MCU by USB C, containing the RPi CM4 externally.
  • Added cutout for a internal power supply (future upgrade option)
  • Added new supporting extrusion to prevent filament scraps from getting inside the printer.
  • Added sentient catgirl A.I.

Positron V3

Released: May 22nd, 2022

This is the original release of the Positron printer by KRALYN.

The V3.0 has been officially retired by Positron 3D, and no direct support will be provided for this design, as the latest revision fixes many core issues related to this machine.
