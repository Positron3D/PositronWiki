Assembly 04 - Bed V Holder

CAD Assembly

Required Parts

  • 2x Thumbscrew, M3x12
  • 1x Bed V Holder [CNC]
  • 2x Hex Female Standoff, M3x22
  • 2x Machine Screw, Wafer Head, M3x3
  • 2x Spring, 0.8x8x10mm
  • 2x Bed Spring Retainer Ring (Printed)

Installation

Step 1 - Install Thumb Screws

Required Parts

  • 2x Thumbscrew, M3x12
  • 1x Bed V Holder [CNC]

Assembly

Insert the M3x12 Thumbscrews into the holes located at the tips of the Bed Holder.

Step 2 - Assemble Standoffs

Required Parts

  • 2x Hex Female Standoff, M3x22
  • 2x Machine Screw, Wafer Head, M3x3
  • 2x Spring, 0.8x8x10mm
  • 2x Bed Spring Retainer Ring (Printed)

Assembly

  1. Screw the Wafer Head Screws into the Standoffs
  2. Slip the Bed Spring Retainer Ring over the Standoffs so that the screw overhangs the Ring (As Seen Below)
  3. Place the Spring on the assembly, resting above the Bed Spring Retainer Ring

Step 3 - Fasten the Standoffs

Assembly

  • Screw the Standoffs into the Thumbscrews of the Bed Holder
  • Ensure the gaps on the Bed Spring Retention Rings are facing each other.

Assembly Complete
